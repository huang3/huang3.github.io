Interview QA for Machine Learning Position

Oct 21, 2016

Here I list a set of Machine Learning questions & answers for interview who wants to get a data scientist job or similar position.

Question List: * Introduce one of your favorite machine learning algorithms in detail. * How would you evaluate the performance of your classifier? Name two ways at least. * How would you understand overfitting and what you can do about it? * Tell me how to use cross validation for model selection. * What’s the difference of ROC and PRC? * What is cross entropy and how to derive it?
